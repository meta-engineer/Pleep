Here's how Pleep works:

  • Users post responses to threads auto-generated by Pleep or created by other users.
  • Each thread has a timeout period after creation until it locks.
  • At that time the three highest rated posts will recieve Pleep clout on their accounts.
  • After the thread locks it will remain visible for another short period to show off your success.
  • Then the thread and all the posts made on it are permenantly deleted.
The strategy for success is up to you. Esoteric references? Low effort memes? Niche talents? Political instigation? High quality content? Boobs?

We're excited to see where this adventure takes us, its untamed wilderness and we're giving no promises.
